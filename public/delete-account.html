Akkauntni o'chirish

Oxirgi yangilanish: 2026-yil 17-iyul • Account & data deletion (English below)

Xposter — choyxona va restoranlar uchun kassa (POS) ilovasi. Ushbu sahifa akkauntingizni va u bilan bog'liq barcha ma'lumotlarni qanday o'chirishni tushuntiradi.

1. Ilova ichida o'chirish (tavsiya etiladi)

  1. Ilovaga kiring.
  2. Ещё (Yana) bo'limini oching.
  3. Pastdagi «Удалить аккаунт» tugmasini bosing.
  4. Egalar uchun: tasdiqlash uchun УДАЛИТЬ so'zini yozing va «Удалить навсегда» ni bosing.

O'chirish darhol va butunlay amalga oshiriladi.

2. Emaildan so'rov orqali o'chirish

Ilovaga kira olmasangiz, ro'yxatdan o'tgan email manzilingizdan bizga xat yozing: jafaralituraev@gmail.com (mavzu: «Akkauntni o'chirish»). So'rovni 3 ish kuni ichida bajaramiz.

3. Qanday ma'lumot o'chiriladi

Egasi akkauntini o'chirganda quyidagilar butunlay va qaytarilmas o'chiriladi:

  • Akkaunt (Firebase Authentication — email);
  • Zavedeniye (restoran) va uning barcha xodimlari kirishi;
  • Cheklar, tranzaksiyalar, moliyaviy hisobotlar;
  • Menyu, sklad, retseptlar, yetkazib beruvchilar;
  • Mijozlar, sodiqlik (bonus) ma'lumotlari;
  • Zallar, stollar va boshqa sozlamalar.

Xodim o'z akkauntini o'chirsa — faqat uning kirishi va shaxsiy yozuvi o'chiriladi.

4. Ma'lumotni saqlash muddati

O'chirishdan so'ng ma'lumotlar faol tizimdan darhol olib tashlanadi. Zaxira nusxalari (backup) Google Firebase siyosati bo'yicha cheklangan muddat ichida avtomatik tozalanadi. Qonun talab qilgan hollarda (masalan, soliq hisoboti) ayrim yozuvlar zaruriy muddatgacha saqlanishi mumkin.

English summary

To delete your Xposter account and all associated data, open the app, go to Ещё (More) and tap “Удалить аккаунт” (Delete account); owners confirm by typing УДАЛИТЬ. If you cannot access the app, email jafaralituraev@gmail.com from your registered address and we will complete the request within 3 business days. Deleting an owner account permanently removes the account, the venue and its staff logins, and all receipts, transactions, financial reports, menu, inventory, suppliers, clients, loyalty data and settings. Deleting an employee account removes only that login and personal record. Data is erased from live systems immediately; backups are purged automatically under Google Firebase's retention policy, except records we must keep to comply with law (e.g. tax reporting).
